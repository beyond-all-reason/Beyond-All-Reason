SENSEJv1.0 Patch Notes
=======================

Purpose
-------
Normalizes faction sensor, radar, sonar, and jammer units for competitive play.
Also updates Juno costs and adds the Cortex Forge combat engineer.

T1 Jammer Towers
----------------
Affected: Armada Jamtower (armjamt), Cortex Jamtower (corjamt), Legion Jammer (legjam)
- Normalized to the Armada baseline.
- Cost: 240 metal / 8,500 energy
- Build time: 9,950
- Jamming range: 500
- Energy upkeep: 40

T2 Radar Bots
-------------
Affected: Armada Marky (armmark), Cortex Voyeur (corvoyr), Legion radar bot (legaradk)
- Normalized to the Cortex baseline.
- Cost: 99 metal / 1,350 energy; build time: 5,000
- Radar 2,200; sight 925; health 390; speed 45
- Acceleration 0.05635; braking 0.2; turn rate 670.45001

T2 Jammer Bots
--------------
Affected: Armada Eraser (armaser), Cortex Spectre (corspec), Legion jammer bot (legajamk)
- Normalized to the Armada baseline, with speed reduced to 40.5.
- Cost: 78 metal / 1,400 energy; build time: 6,000
- Jamming range 450; upkeep 80; sight 380; health 340
- Acceleration 0.138; braking 0.5175; turn rate 1,201.75

-----------------

Cortex Forge
------------
Affected: Cortex T2 vehicle plant (coravp), Cortex Forge (corforge)
- Adds corforge to the Cortex T2 vehicle plant build list.
- Explicitly loads corforge when it is not already available in standard games.
- Replaces the Forge flamethrower's combat and visual behavior with Dragon's Maw values.
- Uses Dragon's Maw burst, interval, reload, AoE, edge effectiveness, spray,
  velocity, flame colors, intensity, growth, texture, and effects.
- Flamethrower range reduced from 410 to 325.
- Damage per flame: 10.8 default, 16.2 commanders, 2.7 submarines.
- Theoretical default DPS: approximately 157.1.

Activation
----------
These repository files do not activate the tweak automatically. Load the contents of
SENSEJv1.0.base64.txt through BAR's lobby TweakDefs option or a saved lobby preset.
